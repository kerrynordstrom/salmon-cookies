Hey! If you've been brooding about the next flavors in sweet treats, you've come to the right place. We've been in the Pacific Nortwest for several generations and know a thing or two about our hook-nosed friends, the salmon. We found a way to process salmon so fine that when mixed with cookie dough is nearly untraceable. Salmon cookies are a great way to get your day's protein and Omega-3 fatty acids when you're treating yourself!
